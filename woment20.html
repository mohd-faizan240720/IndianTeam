T20 International Cricket

Brief History

The Indian women's cricket team played its first T20 International match in 2006 against England. Their most significant achievement in this format came in 2020 when they reached the finals of the ICC Women's T20 World Cup, showcasing their talent and competitiveness on the global stage. Since then, the team has continued to grow, consistently ranking among the top teams in women's T20 cricket and inspiring many young players across the country.
