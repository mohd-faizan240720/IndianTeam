T20 International Cricket

Brief History

India played its first T20 International match in 2006 against South Africa. The team's biggest achievement in this format came in 2007 when they won the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 under the captaincy of Mahendra Singh Dhoni. Since then, India has been a formidable force in T20 cricket, consistently ranking among the top teams in the world.
