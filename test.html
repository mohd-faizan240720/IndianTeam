Test Cricket

Brief History

India played its first Test match on June 25, 1932, against England at Lord's. The team had to wait until 1952 for its first Test victory, against England in Madras (now Chennai). Over the years, India has produced some of the greatest Test cricketers, including Sunil Gavaskar, Kapil Dev, Sachin Tendulkar, and Rahul Dravid. In 2009, India reached the number one ranking in Test cricket for the first time.

Key Statistics for India in Test Cricket

  • Highest team total: 759/7d vs England (2016)
  • Lowest team total: 36 vs Australia (2020)
  • Most runs: Sachin Tendulkar (15,921 runs)
  • Highest individual score: Virender Sehwag (319 vs South Africa, 2008)
  • Most wickets: Anil Kumble (619 wickets)
  • Best bowling figures in an innings: Anil Kumble (10/74 vs Pakistan, 1999)
